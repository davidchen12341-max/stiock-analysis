With $31-35B in additional 2026 CapEx, free cash flow will be deeply negative. If credit markets tighten, interest rates rise, or revenue growth disappoints, refinancing risk becomes existential. Net debt/EBITDA is currently 5.5x; if adjusted EBITDA growth stalls, this metric deteriorates rapidly. Multiple lawsuits alleging infrastructure scaling misrepresentation add tail risk.

**Probability:** Medium (25% likelihood of a credit-related stress event or guidance cut forcing equity raise within 24 months)
**Downside Impact:** A forced equity raise at distressed prices would be highly dilutive. In a severe scenario, stock could revisit $63.80 (52-week low) or lower.
**Mitigation:** 56% adjusted EBITDA margin in Q1 2026 shows the underlying unit economics are sound; contracted backlog provides cash flow predictability for lenders; NVIDIA's strategic stake provides credibility.
